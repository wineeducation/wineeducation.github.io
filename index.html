About

When I lived in France, a friend of mine would teach me about wine while we sat around the table, cooked meals, and hung out on the patio. The experience marked me deeply, and it's one I hope to share with you. We should all get to learn about wine the way I did, in our own space with those we want there with us.

Later, studying wine formally, I realized that the wine industry has failed the consumer, pedagogically. Instead of focusing on how to taste and assess wine, give language to those ideas, and to learn to communicate those ideas in a way that makes sense to industry professionals, we have, explicitly and implicitly, told them that they should memorize wine regions, know what loam is and why it's important, and that they're inadequate if they can't blind taste wine.

What keeps people from getting into wine isn't simple snobbery or gatekeeping, but a lack of empowerment. That's what I'm passionate about correcting.

In-Home Tastings

A guided wine tasting event in your own home with those you want there with you. Protected from ascot wearing wine snobs asking, "Do I detect a hint of boysenberry?" You will learn everything you need to know to be self-confident in your own ability... and you'll learn what things are and are not essential.

This is a bespoke experience, completely customizable to your tastes. There really is nothing else like it. We will discuss your vision for the event ahead of time, so that you, your friends, and your guests will have a remarkable experience.
