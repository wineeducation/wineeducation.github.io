WINE EDUCATION

When I lived in France, a friend of mine would teach me about wine while we sat around the table, cooked meals, and hung out on the patio. The experience marked me deeply, and it's one I hope to share with you.

My completely customizable in-home wine class will give you everything you need to browse wine lists with and easy confidence and feel at home in any wine shop, and it all happens in your space with those you want there with you. I think everyone should get to learn about wine the way I did, so I invite you to invite me into your home for a one-of-a-kind event.
