About Me

When I lived in France, a friend of mine would teach me about wine while we sat around the table, cooked meals, and hung out on the patio. The experience marked me deeply, and it's one I hope to share with you.

Studying wine formally, I realized the wine industry has failed the consumer by not having a knowledge base tailored to their needs. This has discouraged a lot of people from getting into wine, and that's what I've set out to change through my guided wine tasting course.

In just an hour or two, I teach the essentials and demystify the other stuff so that you can have confidence in yourself at the restaurant and in the winery. There really is nothing like it anywhere else. Best of all, I come to you. You get to learn about wine in the comfort of your own space with those you want there with you, just like I did.

Details

I'm going to be honest with you. This is first and foremost a passion project. I think wine should be for everyone so I'm trying not to price anyone out. I charge $50 per person, plus the cost wine, which I think is a great value for a private, tailored, in-home wine event in Santa Barbara.

I work with you and a local wine shop to choose the bottles for you to pick up. From there, the freedom is yours. Serve a meal, lounge on the couch, or throw a party. I'll work with you to tailor the event to your vision.
